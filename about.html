Our Mission

A better world is a kinder world.

About

Our world is divided, especially to those who are different than we are or have different opinions than ones we carry. It’s always been this way, and the divided nature does ebb and flow throughout history, but our country is divided now in a way that many of us have not seen in our lifetimes.

Let’s work on this together.
Together we can create a world where kindness outshines hatred. This is the inspiration behind The DailyDeed Project, spreading kindness and cheer to all.

How Does It work?

The daily deeds that we are sharing with you on our webpage are an attempt to spread some kindness both at home and away from home. For each daily task you complete, you can submit your stories/pictures to appear on our Sharing is Caring page (as space allows) and you get a virtual prize in addition to the joy from just completing your DailyDeed. Currently, the DailyDeed Project is just that: a web development project for the Commonwealth Coders program. But the dream is to make this project more "real" with real sponsors and funding, enough to have not only virtual prizes but real ones as well. Join our newsletter for updates.
